Specs and Features

Tech Specs

  • Resolution: 1,440 x 1,600 per eye (2,880 x 1,600 total)
  • Display Type: Diamond PenTile OLED
  • Refresh Rate: 72hz
  • Field of View: ~110 degrees diagonal (~95-100 degrees horizontal)
  • IPD Adjustment: Hardware
  • Tracking: 4 camera inside-out 6DoF tracking of headset and controllers
  • CPU/SOC: Snapdragon 835
  • Audio: Built-in microphone, stereo audio inside headstrap, and dual stereo 3.5mm headphone jacks
  • Storage: onboard 64GB or 128GB ‚Äî no expandable storage
  • Wi-Fi: 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band
  • Connections: USB-C
  • Weight: 570 grams (subject to change)

Features/Additional Notes

  • Low-latency, stereo-correct video passthrough
  • Multi-room "Guardian" that remembers the configured playspace boundaries for several different rooms
  • Will be able to track in 6DoF in well-lit indoor environments ("bright enough to read a book")
  • Able to side-load apps
  • Screen-casting/mirroring

What is the Oculus Quest?

The Quest is Oculus' first stand-alone virtual reality headset featuring 6 degree of freedom (6DoF) inside-out tracking with two 6DoF controllers. This means that you can experience VR while moving around your room with no computer, wires, tethers, or external sensors. You're able to put the Quest on and jump into VR immediately with no setup or additional equipment required.

When will the Quest be Available?

The Oculus Quest released on May 21st, 2019 and can now be purchased. Please refer to the above link.

How much will the Quest Cost?

The Oculus Quest is available in two storage configurations: 64GB and 128GB. The 64GB Quest retails for $399 USD. The 128GB version retails for $499.
